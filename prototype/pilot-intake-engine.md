# Pilot Intake Engine (PIE)

## What It Is

The Pilot Intake Engine (PIE) is the structured intake layer for AISA's early Decision Intelligence workflow.

Its purpose is to capture real-world decision scenarios in a consistent format so they can be prepared for simulation, analysis, and structured output.

Rather than beginning with abstract theory, PIE begins with practical questions from builders, operators, and capital providers.

---

## Why It Matters

Many important decisions are still made through:

- intuition
- fragmented discussion
- incomplete stakeholder input
- backward-looking analytics
- static planning documents

PIE is designed to convert decision uncertainty into a simulation-ready input.

Examples include:

- Which funding structure should we use?
- How should contributors be rewarded?
- How should a project coordinate without hierarchy?
- What risks emerge under a given ownership or reputation model?

---

## Role in the Stack

PIE sits at the front of the Decision Intelligence workflow.

### Basic flow

1. User submits a decision scenario
2. Inputs are structured into simulation-ready variables
3. Scenario is processed through an initial simulation layer
4. Outputs are translated into decision-grade insights

---

## Initial Scope

The first version of PIE is intentionally lean.

Its purpose is to validate the workflow, not to over-engineer the interface.

Initial goals:

- capture clear decision questions
- identify key variables and constraints
- support early simulation scenarios
- provide a reliable bridge into Hamlet-1

---

## Example Intake Areas

The intake process may include:

- decision type
- objective or intended outcome
- contributors involved
- timeframe
- constraints
- possible models or options being compared
- known risks or uncertainties
- desired output format

---

## Relationship to Hamlet-1

Hamlet-1 is the first simulation layer within AICitySim.

PIE prepares the structured inputs required for Hamlet-1 to run useful early decision scenarios.

Together, PIE + Hamlet-1 form the first practical step toward a broader Decision Intelligence system.

---

## Current Status

PIE is currently in active development.

The immediate focus is:

- lean interface structure
- useful schema for decision scenarios
- reliable preparation of inputs for early simulation runs

Screenshots and interface previews will be added as the prototype matures.
